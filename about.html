About us

Let us ourselves

        Irek and Deborah are two fresh computer science student in Griffith College. Beside sharing the same passion for programming, they are also passionate on watersports. Irek is specialised in scuba diving. He started diving 4 years ago during a trip in Malesia. He always describes going under water as an adreline kick. Deborah prefer stay above the water. Her balancing skills and experience gave her power over the water.

Why did we choose malta?

        We believe Malta is a beautiful country. And not only becuase we can combine our favourite sports, windsurf and scuba diving. Malta is full of life and attractions and not only. Everyone can find something suitable in this place, from exciting sport activities to quiet and relaxing spots. Another advantage of malta is weather, summer is best time if you are looking for beach and sun holiday, then winter if you want to escape from freezing moths as Malta is still relatively warm.
