Why practice scuba diving in Malta?

There are so many reasons why scuba diving is such a fan! Let me introduce few of them, and the rest you will discover once you try. When you are under water, you leave your day to day busy life behind.This adventure helps you forget about keep calling phone cell or emergency emails that you always have to reply. Just relax and let me show you what underwater world of Malta have in offer for you.

The best scuba diving spots

You can find listed below one of the best marine sites you can find in Malta.

The Blue Hole

The blue hole is great experience for everyone! For less advanced divers shallow waters down to 15 meters, are full of colorful life, you can meet there creatures as bream, moray eels, octopi, lobster, and colorful parrot-fish. For more advanced and brave diviers blue hole holds indescribable secrets on 35 meters deep. Wanna know what's there? Go and chceck!

The Blenheim Bomber wreck

Most exclusive offer of Maltan Underwater world is definatelly The Blenheim Bomber wreck. This place have been visited by only small group of people. Why? Because for most of divier schools exact location is unknown. The Bristol Blenheim is a British light bomber aircraft. It crashed on Malta during world war II and now its a favourite meeting spot of turltles.

Inland Sea tunnel

This spot is located near the Blue Hole and is over 80 meters long tunner. This location is bit dangerous for beginners as boats are following the same way, so make sure you are always very careful! Once you enter the tunnel the light effects created by the sunlight make a good job and you feels like youwanna stay there forever, but dont stop there is much more! Next you need to use a torch to keep exploring the walls. I wont tell you everything lets leave the rest as surprise.
